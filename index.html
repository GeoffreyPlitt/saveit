SaveIt

Configure your webhook settings to start saving URLs from Android

How SaveIt Works

  • Install this PWA on your Android device
  • Share content from any app to SaveIt
  • SaveIt sends the content to your webhook
  • Configure your webhook URL and Bearer token below

Installation Guide (Android Only)

Note: This app only works as a share target on Android devices.

  1. Open this page in Chrome on your Android device
  2. Tap the menu (three dots) in the upper right
  3. Select "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
  4. After installation, SaveIt will appear in your Android share menu

Webhook Setup

Your webhook endpoint must:

  • Accept POST requests with JSON data
  • Allow CORS requests from https://geoffreyplitt.github.io
  • Accept Bearer token authentication
  • Process the following payload format:
{
  "title": "Page Title",
  "text": "Selected Text",
  "url": "https://example.com/page",
  "timestamp": "2025-05-31T10:15:30.123Z"
}
This token will be stored locally on your device only
